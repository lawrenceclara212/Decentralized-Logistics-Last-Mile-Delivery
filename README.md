# Decentralized Logistics Last-Mile Delivery

## Overview

This repository implements a blockchain-based system for managing last-mile delivery operations in a decentralized and transparent manner. By leveraging smart contracts, this solution provides improved visibility, efficiency, and accountability throughout the final stage of the delivery process, connecting carriers, merchants, and customers in a trustless environment.

## Core Contracts

### Carrier Verification Contract
Validates and maintains records of legitimate delivery service providers:
- Carrier identity verification and onboarding
- Vehicle registration and compliance tracking
- Driver credentials and performance metrics
- Insurance and liability verification
- Service level agreement parameters

### Package Registration Contract
Records essential information about shipments entering the last-mile network:
- Shipment dimensions and weight
- Handling requirements (fragile, temperature-sensitive)
- Delivery priority and time windows
- Customer contact information
- Special instructions and delivery preferences

### Route Optimization Contract
Manages efficient delivery sequencing and resource allocation:
- Dynamic route planning algorithms
- Capacity utilization optimization
- Traffic and weather condition integration
- Fuel efficiency calculations
- Multi-carrier coordination for load balancing

### Proof of Delivery Contract
Documents successful package handoffs and delivery verification:
- Timestamped delivery confirmation
- Digital signature or biometric verification
- Photo documentation of delivery
- Exception handling for failed deliveries
- Customer satisfaction tracking

## Implementation

The system connects with existing logistics platforms, GPS tracking systems, and mobile applications to provide seamless integration into operational workflows. Smart contracts automate verification processes, payments, and dispute resolution while maintaining an immutable record of all delivery activities.

## Benefits

- Enhanced transparency for all stakeholders
- Reduced disputes and delivery exceptions
- Improved carrier accountability and performance
- Automated settlement and payment processes
- Real-time tracking and visibility
- Secure and tamper-proof delivery records
